Quality Materials for CCG & MTG Collectors

Power 9 Pro spent months examining different materials, scrutinizing plastics for scratch resistance, flexibility and durability. We also wanted a solution that wouldn't damage our cards.

The final product is the N-Dexers: scratch resistant, transparent and gentle on cards. They are also lightweight and thin so they won't add excessive space and weight to your already crowded storage containers.

N-Dexers Dimensions

  • Sized for Standard Boxes
  • Staggered tabs aligned on both sides of the N-Dexers
  • Organize your cards either horizontally or vertically
  • Main dimensions: 3.5" x 2.5" x .25mm — Just slightly larger than a card.
  • Tab dimensions: 1/8" x 15/16"
